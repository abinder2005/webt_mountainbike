Fabio Wibmer

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The austrian supertalent Fabio Wibmer is even for non-mountainbikers a term. Having the most succesful bicycle videos on Youtube with some over 100 million views it´s not surprising that he´s one of the biggest german speaking youtubers. He is also one of the most paid mountainbikers with being youtuber, being sponsored by Red Bull and Specialized, and having an own clothing brand "Sick Series. Fabio rides bikes since he was a little child. Back then he only owned a trial bike wich is still his main bike. Later he got his first downhill bike and started racing as a side activity to the trial but because he is so good on any bike he soon became one of the best in the world on both bikes."

  1. He is 25 years old
  2. Sponsored by Red Bull and Specialized
  3. Nickname is Whipmer
  4. Austrian downhill champion 2016
  5. Biggest achievement is a double backflip on a downhill bike

Loic Bruni

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Loic Bruni is a French top tier downhill racer who won the 2019 world cup overall and was downhill word champion 3 times in a row. Through that and due to his many cooperations with Red Bull where he got his own show "Fast Life". He is porbaply the most famous downhill racer right now. Like Fabio Wibmer he rides for Specialized bikes wich most likely is the best bike brand right now. When Bruni was 10 years old he got hisd first bike. After a few succesful races in his youth Specialized noticed him and took him to their team. There he soon got to the top of the races where he still is today.

  1. 29 years old
  2. lives in Andorra
  3. sponsored by Red Bull and Specialized
  4. 4 times world champ and overall winner in downhill
  5. races Cross Country for fun in his off season

Andreu Lacondeguy

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Andreu Lacondeguy is a category for himself. He is probaply the most extreme and maybe even the best rider in the world. He is the Red Bull Rampage winner of 2014 and attends the Fest series( Events ) regularly. In 2019 he unfortunately crashed in both of his runs in rampage but still won the best line contest. He also holds the record for the highest drop ever jumped off (around 27 meters). He was born in spain in the year 1989 and ten years later he won his first bike contest!

  1. Is from Spain
  2. Rampage winner
  3. Was founder of the Fest series
  4. Sponsored by Red Bull and Commencal bicycles

Sam Hill

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Samuel Hill is an Australian mountainbike legend who was born in 1985. Back in time he was a downhill biker who won championship in 2006, 2007 and 2010 and also won overall world cup 2 times. Later in 2017 he decided to go with enduro biking where he also was on top of the game. In 2019 he won the EWS overall.

  1. Sponsored by Nukeproof
  2. Is from Australia
  3. He races enduro and downhill
  4. multiple dh champion
