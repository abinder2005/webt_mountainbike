Mtb Events

The most extreme and spectacular riding you for sure will find on mtb events. The most exciting will be listed down there.

Red Bull Rampage

One of the most popular events is the Red Bull Rampage. This crazy show is yearly held in Utah Usa and was first carried out in 2001 and was won by Wade Simmons.

in this competition it´s all about building and later riding the craziest line possible. That doing a frontflip over a canyon or jumping off a 20 meter cliff are normal things to see there.

The riders are flewn up the rampage mountain by a helicopter and the judges who sit down in the valley give them points for tricks, speed and style.

Fest Series

Another great show to watch is the Fest series it´s an event where the best freerider in the world come together to jump and do tricks on some of the biggest jumps there are.

The people there are famous people like Andreu Lacondeguy ( Riders ) or Adolf Silver. Even tough there are jumps about 40 meters long noone luckily has died there.

UCI World Cup

The Formula one of MTB is the UCI World Cup there are 2 categories competing in the World Cup. Downhill and Enduro. Every year they travel around the world to 8 of the most demanding tracks they can compete on. Every race gives points for qualification and race result the overall winner of a wc season goes in the history books of mountainbiking. After a season there is 1 special race which decides who will be the world champion of this year. In 2020 in Dh men this is Reece Wilson.

Enduro World Series

The EWS is like the UcI World Cup but with enduro bikes. So there are no 5 minute races every weekend it are like 1 hour races in stages. One of the most successful riders there is Sam Hill. ( Riders )
