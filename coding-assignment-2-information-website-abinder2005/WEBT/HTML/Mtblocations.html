Mtb locations

Around the World many people call themself mountainbiker. This means that almost everywhere there is a place to ride your bike. On this Website there will be listed the best and craziest places to ride.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Whistler Britsh Columbia

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Whistler mountains for most mountainbikers is the "Mekka" of Mtb. Wich is no wonder with its almost 100 trails you can reach with a cablecar and 700 more accesable by a climb. From Xc to Slopestyle every Mtb Category can have fun here.

The trails reach from beginner trails, to legendary trails like the A-Line, to Pro lines like Goats Gully

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Utah Usa

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This beautiful place isn´t only good for hiking and taking pictures of, it´s also a mtb heaven. The best way to go there for normal people is with an enduro bike ( MTB-Types ) because there are no shuttles out there and most of the trails are still pretty demanding

But Utah for sure isn´t famous as a mtb region for the nice view, there´s a reason why the most extrem mtb event that exists is held up here. The Red Bull Rampage. That makes Utah a dreamplace for every Prorider and crazy freerider.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Leogang

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The biggest local Bikepark is "The epic Bikepark Leogang". It has 13 trails and a Bikeplayground. Technically you could say it´s linke with the Bikepark Saalbach-Hinterglemm wich would make it the biggest Bikepark in Europe, but thats not official. Leogang has amazing trails and beautiful sights. One of those trails is the "Speedster" where the Downhill Worldcupis held on. Since 2020 also the Cross Country world cup is carried out ther too.( Events ).

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Innsbruck

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Here in Austria, more precisely in Innsbruck also Crankworx takes place. This is seen as the biggest Mtb festival in the world and riders from everywhere come there to compete.( Events )

For this event there was built one of the biggest slopestyle courses in the world the "crankworx Innsbruck jumps". Next to it there´is also a beautiful bikepark called Mutters wich are Fabio Wibmers Hometrails( Riders )

In the opposite direction, there sits the famous Nordkette, where one of the most difficult trails of europe is on.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Lenzerheide

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

One of the most beautiful bikepark in the world is Lenzerheide with almost 3000 meters height as a starting point and the beautiful Heidsee in the valley it´s on the to do list of every european mountainbiker.

The reason why Lenzerheide is so famous amongst mountainbikers is because of the xc an dh worldcups there are every year.
